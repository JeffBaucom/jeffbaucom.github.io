Tekstyles

Tekstyles is the only freestyle dance organization at Georgia Tech, since becoming president in 2013, I have restructured the club dramatically:

  • Redesigned practice schedule, geared towards engaging practices that make it easy to join the club
  • Created The Summit
  • Created South Bound
  • Created the mentor system for helping newer dancers gain a foothold while encouraging experienced dancers to challenge themselves
  • Oversaw the adoption of a committee system to encourage members to get involved with the management of the organization

South Bound

South Bound is a dance competition held annually at Georgia Tech by Tekstyles. My goal in creating South Bound was to emulate the events I had witnessed in other regions of the country, whose presence was lacking from the landscape of the Atlanta dance scene. South Bound is one of a kind in terms of both judge and DJ selection and the diversity of styles represented.

The Summit

Created by Buzzbeats, Georgia Tech’s beatboxing organization and Tekstyles, Georgia Tech’s freestyle dance organization, The Summit is a semesterly hip hop event that serves as a gathering of artists in the Georgia Tech community that associate themselves with hip hop culture.

We are currently preparing for the fourth edition of The Summit, on September 17th, 2015 in the Georgia Tech Student Center Ballroom. This time, Georgia Tech’s Student Center Programs Council has joined the planning team alongside BuzzBeats and Tekstyles. In the past two editions of The Summit, we have encouraged participation from other colleges and the general public.

The Summit culminates in tournament-style beatbox and all-styles dance battles. Judged by talented local dancers and beatboxers, The Summit provides a competitive outlet not only for dancers and beatboxers at Georgia Tech but for the entire Atlanta hip hop community.
